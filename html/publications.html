Is breathing rate a confound for power EEG-BCIs?

Brain computer interfaces (BCIs) help people communicate directly from their brain impulses with a computer. Breathing rate could be a factor that compromises this communication. We train healthy participants to adapt their breathing rate and find whether or not it impacts the power in different frequency bands.
